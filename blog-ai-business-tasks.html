AI Automation

Five Business Tasks You Can Simplify With AI

Practical AI use begins with repetitive, structured business work.

AI is most useful when it supports a clear process rather than replacing judgement.

Useful starting points include drafting follow-up emails, summarising meetings, generating first-draft content, organising FAQs and converting repeated activities into SOPs.

Each workflow should include human review, data privacy safeguards and a clear owner.

Start small, measure time saved and improve the workflow before expanding automation.
