Image & Legacy

How to Build a Professional Legacy

A professional legacy is built by documenting values, milestones, lessons and impact.

A legacy is more than visibility. It is the structured record of what a person or organisation stood for and contributed.

Begin by documenting the founder story, important milestones, turning points, achievements, values and lessons.

Convert this material into a biography, timeline, archive, interview series, website and long-term digital repository.

The strongest legacy assets are authentic, organised and useful to future generations.
